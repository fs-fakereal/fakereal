VISION

Be aware and be informed.
Be in charge of your safety and life.

FakeReal is dedicated to raising awareness about deepfakes and AI-generated images, empowering everyday people to easily identify them. As technology evolves, so does our need to understand its impact. We offer a free service to detect deepfakes and AI-generated images, providing clear explanations alongside our results. Our goal is to simplify deepfake identification and reporting.
